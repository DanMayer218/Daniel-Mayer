Who is Daniel Mayer?

Daniel Mayer is a recent graduate from a Coding Bootcamp through Columbia University's Engineering Department. As an artist, I have an innate sense of design. As a former small business owner I have great leadership skills in project management, terrific skills as a communicator both directly with clients and delegating responsibility to my team.

Thank You!

Thank you for your interest and taking the time to learn a bit more about the skills and qualities I can bring to your organization.

I am a recent graduate from the Coding Bootcamp at Columbia University's Fu Foundation of Engineering and Applied Science. There I learned a broad variety of computer languages through lectures, at home assignments and larger group projects and presentations.

My skills come from real world experience as a former small business owner. My company designed and built gardens exclusively in Manhattan for an elite clientele. I have strong communication skills as I did all the estimating, sales and communication with my clients. Communication was also paramount to my leadership skills managing projects and the delegation of responsibilities to employees. In addition to communication and management, I consider my skills as a designer and creative powerhouse to be amongst my greatest assets. I have a great work ethic, never met an obstacle I couldn't work through, bring great ideas to the table and bring out the best in those around me.

WORK EXPERIENCE

July 2015 - December 2018

NEW LEAF METROPOLITAN LANDSCAPING

Owner

I purchased the company I previously worked for. New Leaf Metropolitan Landscaping designed, built and maintained public, private and residential gardens for elite clientele in New York City. My responsibilities as the owner were all client communications, designing, estimating, sales and billing. Organizing, scheduling and delegating all work to employees (2 field crews). Hiring, firing, payroll and all other administrative roles.

March 2005 - December 2014

New Leaf Landscape design and contracting

Project Manager

For 5 years prior to purchasing New Leaf Landscape design and contracting, I was responsible for Project management, client interactions, estimating, sales, organizing and scheduling my projects amongst many other responsibilities. I studied horticulture and landscape design at the Bronx Botanical Gardens and learned Sketchup Pro, Adobe photoshop and other graphic softwares which were implemented in my design work.

July 2002 - June 2005

Rick Rowe Mastering

Audio Engineer

I worked as an audio engineer under the supervision of my mentor Rick Rowe. I did work recording musicians, Mastering and digitizing albums before being sent out to manufacturers for mass production. Travelled to South Africa to operate the sound boards with Rick Rowe for Miriam Makeba on her tour across the country. Other notable clients, the Rock and Roll hall of fame.
